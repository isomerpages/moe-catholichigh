**Congratulations to our Chinese teacher, Mr Ng Seng Hong, for receiving the 19th Inspiring Chinese Language Teachers Awards 2016  (全国模范华文教师奖) !** 

Mr Ng joins our Chinese Senior Teacher Mr Liu Changyu and Chinese teacher Ms Cai Xiaona as our 3 Inspiring Chinese Language Teacher Awards recipients. We are proud of our teachers’ efforts to inspire our students to develop an appreciation for all disciplines, including Chinese language and culture!

##### About the Inspiring Chinese Language Teachers Awards:

##### Endorsed by the Ministry of Education, the 19th Inspiring Chinese Language Teachers Awards is organised by Lianhe Zaobao, co-organised by Singapore Chinese Middle School Teachers’ Association and Singapore Chinese  
Teachers’ Union, and sponsored by Lee Foundation. The objective of the awards is to give due recognition to teachers who have displayed exemplary efforts in the teaching of Chinese language and culture in Singapore. These Chinese teachers have also, over the past year, inspired their students to develop appreciation for the Chinese language and culture.
